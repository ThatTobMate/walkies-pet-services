Welcome To Walkies Pet Care Services

We Are Here To Cater For Your Pets Every Need

Remember a tired dog is a happy dog!

Dog walking

Monday to Friday we can give you a daily dog walking service. We adore dogs here, so you can rest assured your dogs will be extremely well looked after.

Cat Feeding

We will feed your cats, up to 2 times per day, while you are away or unable to feed them.

Additional Services

We will look after your property and turn lights on and off accordingly, draw curtains when needed, bring in your mail, and water plants, no job is to small just ask when you are making your booking I am sure we can accommodate your every need while you are away, to give you peace of mind to enjoy your break.
