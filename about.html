About us

At Walkies Pet Care Services we are dedicated to helping care for your pets, so whether you need help due to work commitments, holidays, a day out or because you are feeling under the weather, you can trust us for all your pet care needs!

Our friendly pet carers and dog walkers are police checked so you can rest assured that your furry friends are safe in our hands.

Lisa

I live in Wickford Essex with my son Connor, Tia my beloved German Shepard. Being passionate about animals has always driven me to wanting to work along side them, so when I fell pregnant with Connor I had to give up my day job, and found walking my dogs hard work whilst pregnant, this made me think that there may be a need for a walking / day care service in our area hence Walkies Pet Care Services was born.


I felt that given my upbringing and a lifelong love of dogs, cats, horses and other animals - I am well placed to offer a professional service which puts you the customer, and your pet first.
